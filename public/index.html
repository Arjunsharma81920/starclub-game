How StarClub Works

  1. Home tab se app ka overview dekho.
  2. Live Room tab pe jao, naam enter karo, message bhejo – sab clients ko real time me dikhega.
  3. Profile tab me apna display name & bio local storage me save kar sakte ho.

Ye sab single-page app me alag-alag screens hain, jo APK ke andar ek hi WebView me smooth kaam karengi.
